Alya Kemi

What is it?

Alya Kemi is Minecraft realm focused on friendship, the community, and building creations that will make their mark on the history page of our time. With more than five members, Alya Kemi has a flourishing economy, safe territory, and plenty of room to grow and build your own creations, no matter their size. Though it is highly encouraged that members contribute to group projects, it is not required and will not affect your membership. All that we ask is that you play nice with others, don't steal, don't kill, and don't destroy. Breaking any one of these may damage the trust in which the members have towards you.

On this site, you will find

  • Pictures from our own creations
  • A trivia for entertainment
  • A way to request a building be built on the realm
  • A way of contacting the owner if you are interested in joining
  • Short paragraphs about adventures in Alya Kemi
  • Reviews that have been left by current members of Alya Kemi
