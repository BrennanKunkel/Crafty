I am General Sarbina - General of a mighty warring tribe that has been completely exterminated by the evil creeper race. When I came to the realm, Alya Kemi, it was but a small town. Thanks to my skills in war and strategy, most of the evil creatures have been put in their proper place: the nether world. I have even managed to tolerate the Alya Kemi leader, Lykos Sarpedon, who continues to challenge my superior wisdom in war and strategy. I can not bash too harshly upon Sarpedon, however, as he has managed to unify many strangers together for a common cause. This is perhaps the greatest wish of any great leader. Though, while I can not say that I would not be able to do the same, I respect his capability and nobility. Though the economy that Sarpedon has established greatly encourages industry, I have not found the need to begin one of my own; nay, I will remain a homeless nomad of the Alya Kemi realm perhaps for the rest of my lonesome life. But I will be happy. Yes, thanks to the Alya Kemi realm, I will remain happy.
